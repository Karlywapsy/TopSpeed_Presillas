Beat the Fast

Set personal goals to beat your previous high score. This will motivate you to improve your tapping speed. While speed is important, accuracy matters too2. Ensure that your taps are consistent and precise.

Greatest Tapper

Tap on the screen as fast as you can. The goal is to tap as many times as possible within the given time limit. When the time is up, your result, which is the number of taps you made, will automatically appear on the screen.

Record your Speed

The results will automatically appear on the screen when the time is up. This will show your tapping speed, often measured in Taps Per Second (TPS) or Clicks Per Second (CPS).
